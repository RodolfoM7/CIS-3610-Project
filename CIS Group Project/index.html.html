The Night of the Sighting

On a warm September evening in 1952, the small town of Flatwoods, West Virginia became the center of one of America's most enduring mysteries.

Timeline of Events

  • 7:15 PM Witnesses saw a bright object streak across the sky.
  • Minutes Later A group walked toward the hillside to investigate.
  • At the Site They reported a strange smell and a tall glowing figure.
  • Afterward The group ran back in fear, spreading the story nationwide.
