The Night of the Sighting

On a warm September evening in 1952, the small town of Flatwoods, West Virginia became the center of one of America's most enduring mysteries.

What Really Happened?

Some believe the event was caused by a meteor, while others argue it was mass hysteria. The truth remains unclear even today.

The Flatwoods Monster has appeared in documentaries, games, and pop culture, becoming one of the most famous cryptids in the U.S.
