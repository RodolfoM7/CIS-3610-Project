What we're about

This website explores the mystery of the Flatwoods Monster, one of America’s most famous cryptid encounters. It follows the timeline of events from the night of the 1952 sighting, detailing the strange lights in the sky, the chilling encounter in the woods, and the aftermath experienced by witnesses. It also examines theories about what may have really happened, while highlighting how the Flatwoods Monster has continued to appear in modern media, inspiring games, stories, and popular culture.

The Night of the Sighting

On a warm September evening in 1952, the small town of Flatwoods, West Virginia became the center of one of America's most enduring mysteries.

What Really Happened?

Some believe the event was caused by a meteor, while others argue it was mass hysteria. The truth remains unclear even today.

In Media

The Flatwoods Monster has appeared in documentaries, games, and pop culture, becoming one of the most famous cryptids in the U.S.
